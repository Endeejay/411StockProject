What is "Historic"?

Historic testing (AKA Backtesting):


The process of applying a trading strategy or analytical method to historical data to see how accurately the strategy or method would have predicted actual results.

How does our historic environment work?

Our historic environment will work similarly to our live environment, we will apply a set of investments over the range of dates that you select.
